Salmon and Zuccini

Description

Marinated salmon with zucchin and squash coins is a healthy and easy dish that will leave you feeling satisfied.

Ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Italian Dressing
  • Zuccini and squash
  • Salt
  • Lemmon Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  1. Marinate salmon for 15 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit. Chop zucchin and squash into coins.
  3. Place marinated salon on baking pan and heat in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  4. Toss sliced zucchin and squash coins in olive oil, salt, and lemmon pepper.
  5. Cook in preheated oven for 10 minutes.
  6. Ensure salmon is cooked to internal temp of 141 degree fahrenheit or above.
  7. Place salmon and veggies on plate and serve.
