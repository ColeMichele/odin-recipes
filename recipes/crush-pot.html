Crushed Potatoes

Description

These crushed potatoes can be made as a side dish or snack. They are seasoned well and make for a delicious salty treat. They can be made ahead of time or with at meal time. Feel free to switch up the seasonings and spices to keep it interesting.

Ingredients:

  • Golden Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Toss potatoes in Olive oil, kosher salt, pepper, onion powder and garlic powder.
  3. Place on baking sheet and roast in preheated oven for 40 minutes.
  4. Remove potatoes from oven and crush with pint glass or potato cursher.
  5. Place back in oven and cook for additional 5-10 minutes til crispy.
